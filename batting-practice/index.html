Motivational Interviewing

Reflection Batting Practice

Step up to the plate and track the client's pitch

Each "pitch" delivers a unique profile of a client alongside a short statement containing a hidden target of change. Formulate an intentional reflection to hit it out of the park. 10 pitches total.

MI Scorecard Matrix:
  • Single Simple Reflection (light mirroring, restating, or rephrasing)
  • Double Affirmation (naming a core strength or value)
  • Triple Complex Reflection (adding deep meaning, reflecting unstated emotion, or feeling reflections)
  • Home Run Change-Oriented Reflection (double-sided reflections or strategies that cultivate change talk/soften sustain talk)
  • Strikeout Non-Adherent (questions, roadblocks, unasked advice, or self-centered tracking)
The speed bonus bar tracks the ball traveling across the plate. Don't stress—the timer is relaxed to give you plenty of space to draft or speak your answer!
