Reflection Batting Practice
Ten pitches, a new change focus each time
Instructions
1
Each pitch is a client statement with its own change focus shown above it.
2
Reflect it back and swing before the speed bonus runs out.
3
The more skillful the reflection, the bigger the hit. Ten pitches, then your box score.
How the swings score:
  • Single Simple reflection (you repeat or rephrase)
  • Double Complex reflection (you add meaning or emotion, including feeling and double-sided reflections)
  • Triple Affirmation (you name a real strength)
  • Home run A reflection that grows the client's change talk or softens their sustain talk
  • Strikeout A question, advice, or praise, not a reflection
The baseball rolling toward home is your speed bonus. Swing while it lasts for extra points. A rushed weak reflection still only earns the weak score, so think first.
