Reflection Batting Practice

Step up to the plate and reflect

A client statement gets pitched to you. Reflect something back, and swing. The better and faster your reflection, the more you score. Ten pitches, then your box score.

How the swings score:
  • Single Simple reflection (you repeat or rephrase)
  • Double Affirmation (you name a real strength)
  • Triple Complex reflection (you add meaning or emotion)
  • Home run Double-sided reflection (you hold both sides)
  • Strikeout A question, advice, or praise about you, not a reflection
The bar under each pitch is your speed bonus. Swing while it lasts for extra points, but a rushed weak reflection still only earns the weak score.
