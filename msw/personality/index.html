With your partner

Share this and talk it through

Take turns with your partner. Read your sentence out loud, then talk through these together.

  • Why did you pick these two traits?
  • Where do they show up in your life?
  • Do the people in your life notice, value, or affirm these traits?

Then listen to your partner read theirs and answer the same. Take it in before you move on.

  • How did it feel to see your own traits turned into something negative?
